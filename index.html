The information we collect is used to:

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please visit our contact page.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
